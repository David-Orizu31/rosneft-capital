# Who we are

About Rosneft Capital

Rosneft Capital is a leading digital asset investment company backed by Rosneft, Russia's largest oil company, founded in 1993. With a strong presence in the energy sector, Rosneft Capital leverages its expertise and resources to provide investors with unparalleled access to the world of cryptocurrency. Our company generates a significant portion of its revenue from oil and gas production, petrochemicals, lubricants, and refining operations, while also exploring opportunities in emerging industries.

At Rosneft Capital, we believe that investing in Bitcoin is a vital component of a diversified investment portfolio. Our platform allows investors to easily access the cryptocurrency market, providing high interest rates and opportunities for growth. We prioritize the security and transparency of all transactions, working closely with regulatory bodies and security agencies to ensure the integrity of our system.

With a deep understanding of the global financial environment and a commitment to excellence, Rosneft Capital has established itself as a trusted player in the cryptocurrency industry. Our team of experts combines international experience with local knowledge, providing investors with a unique perspective on the market. We are proud of our market position and growth, and we look forward to continuing to innovate and expand our services.

Rosneft Capital is also an active participant in the global financial markets, with a presence on major stock exchanges. We believe that the combination of traditional financial instruments and cryptocurrency investment opportunities is key to creating wealth and driving growth. Our goal is to provide investors with a secure, efficient, and user-friendly platform for Bitcoin investment, while promoting economic development and financial inclusion.

Vision

A new era in digital asset investment, leveraging cutting-edge blockchain technology and innovative financial solutions to empower investors and drive growth in the cryptocurrency market. We aspire to establish a secure, efficient, and transparent platform for bitcoin investment, facilitating the widespread adoption of digital assets and redefining the future of wealth creation.

Through our unwavering commitment to excellence, security, and customer-centricity, we are positioned as a trusted leader in the cryptocurrency investment landscape, providing investors with unparalleled opportunities for portfolio diversification, risk management, and returns optimization.

Mission

The only way to ensure our growth is to ensure that our investors grow and flourish first. At Rosneft Capital we deliver sophisticated bitcoin investment solutions that cater to the evolving needs of our clients, while maintaining the highest standards of security, integrity, and transparency. We are dedicated to providing expert guidance, personalized support, and innovative products, including bitcoin trading, mining, and custody solutions, to empower investors and facilitate the attainment of their financial objectives.

By prioritizing client satisfaction, operational excellence, and continuous innovation, we strive to establish enduring relationships with our clients, built on trust, reliability, and mutual benefit. We aim to contribute to the growth and maturation of the cryptocurrency market, while consistently delivering value-added services that exceed our clients' expectations and optimize bitcoin investment outcomes.

# How it Started

Our Origin

Rosneft Capital was founded in 2018 by a team of visionary entrepreneurs and financial experts who recognized the vast potential of digital assets and blockchain technology to transform the global financial landscape. Drawing on the extensive experience in finance, technology, and investment, the founders set out to create a platform that would provide secure, efficient, and transparent bitcoin investment solutions to individuals and institutions worldwide.

With headquarters in Berlin Germany, Rosneft Capital has evolved into a leading player in the cryptocurrency investment space, leveraging cutting-edge technology, innovative products, and expert guidance to empower investors and drive growth in the digital asset market. With commitment to excellence, security, and customer-centricity, Rosneft Capital has established itself as a trusted partner for investors seeking to navigate the complexities of the cryptocurrency market and achieve set financial objectives.

# Account Security

Protection

Our system is designed to safeguard investors' sensitive information with cutting-edge security measures. Our platform employs a multi-layered defense strategy, incorporating advanced firewalls, intrusion detection systems (IDS), and encryption protocols to prevent unauthorized access. With multi-factor authentication (MFA), we verify user identities to ensure secure transactions. Regular security audits and penetration testing help identify vulnerabilities, while our incident response plan enables swift action in case of potential threats. Additionally, our system features robust antivirus software, secure data storage, and real-time monitoring to detect and respond to cyber threats. By prioritizing Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability (CIA), we provide a secure environment for investors to manage their assets with confidence.

Furthermore, our platform utilizes military-grade advanced security measures. SSL/TLS encryption and robust DDoS protection. We prioritize the security of our investors' data and assets.
